01. Problem Statement

Many Pakistani students choose careers with limited guidance, little visibility into their strengths, and no single platform that combines assessment, prediction, explanation, and actionable resources.

Career confusion Resource discovery gap Low explainability Fragmented guidance

02. Objectives

  • Predict suitable careers from academic, aptitude, personality, and interest data.
  • Explain recommendations using SHAP-based reasoning.
  • Provide a chat-based AI counselor for follow-up support.
  • Surface roadmaps, universities, scholarships, courses, and YouTube learning videos.
  • Store sessions, profiles, conversations, and bookmarks securely.

03. Proposed Solution

Assessment Wizard
Collects profile, aptitude, personality, and interests.

Guided Output
Shows ranked careers, confidence, explanation, and roadmap.

The backend combines machine learning, knowledge retrieval, memory, and live web tools to produce a counselor-like experience rather than a static prediction page.

08. Contribution / Innovation

  • Combines predictive ML, explainable AI, and conversational support in one system.
  • Uses Pakistani education context, roadmaps, scholarships, and local university data.
  • Supports both ranked recommendations and follow-up counselor chat.
  • Integrates live web resources such as YouTube videos and course discovery.
  • Provides persistent user data through Supabase and local session memory.
